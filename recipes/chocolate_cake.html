Chocolate cake

Description

This wonderful cake with cherries on top will make you wanna hit the gym the next day!

Ingredients

  • 250g all-purpose flour
  • 400g granulated sugar
  • 75g unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 10g baking powder
  • 9g baking soda
  • 6g fine sea salt
  • 2 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 240ml whole milk
  • 120ml vegetable oil
  • 10ml vanilla extract
  • 240ml boiling water
  • 300g pitted cherries (fresh or drained)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (160°C for fan ovens) and grease your baking pans.
  2. Whisk all dry ingredients in a large bowl until well combined.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; beat with a whisk or mixer for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in boiling water by hand (the batter will be very liquid).
  5. Carefully fold in the pitted cherries.
  6. Bake for 30â€“35 minutes, or until a skewer comes out clean.
  7. Cool in the tin for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
