Omelette

Description

Super easy to make and so good! This omelette has been my daily go to.

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 large eggs
  • 15g unsalted butter
  • 15ml water or whole milk (optional, for extra fluff)
  • 1 pinch fine sea salt
  • 1 pinch black pepper
  • 30g grated cheese or fresh herbs (optional)

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs, salt, pepper, and water/milk in a bowl until no streaks of white remain.
  2. Melt the butter in a small non-stick frying pan over medium-low heat until it foams.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture and let it sit for a few seconds to set at the edges.
  4. Gently push the cooked edges toward the center with a spatula, tilting the pan to let raw egg fill the gaps.
  5. When the top looks slightly wet but mostly set, sprinkle your cheese or herbs over one half.
  6. Fold the omelette in half and slide it onto a plate.
  7. Serve immediately while warm and tender.
