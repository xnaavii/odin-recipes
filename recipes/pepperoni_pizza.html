Pepperoni pizza

Description

This recipe is the best pepperoni pizza out there. If you love cheese and pepperoni, this is for you!

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Oregano (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C
  2. Prepare the base
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the base
  4. Place as much cheese as possible
  5. Pepperoni on top & then sprinkle some oregano
